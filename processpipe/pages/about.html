About Us

Process Pipe has established a national footprint as a wholesaler of carbon and mild steel pipes, fittings and flanges in South Africa, with branches in Gauteng, Durban and Cape Town.

Our Journey

Process Pipe was formed in 1969 and managed with an entrepreneurial flair. The business being a stockist of pipes, fittings and flanges. The business has a footprint in the major provinces of South Africa and a distribution channel that stretches nationwide and across Southern Africa to include Namibia, Botswana, Zimbabwe, Zambia, Eswati, Lesotho and Mozambique.

What We Supply

The Process Pipe product range include carbon steel seamless pipe manufactured to A106 Grade B and A 53 specification, welded SANS 62 PEUC pipe and ERW SANS 719, pipe; BS1387 galvanised threaded and grooved pipe, Japanese Industrial Standard (JIS) fittings, buttweld fittings to specification A 234 WPB; plate & galvanised flanges to SANS 1123, BS 4504, BSEN 1092 and BS 10 and forged flanges to ANSI B16.5, MF & UL approved malleable iron fittings, grooved couplings & fittings.

Process Pipe is also an official distributor of the Arcelor Mittal Seamless Steel pipe range for the petrochemicals sector.
